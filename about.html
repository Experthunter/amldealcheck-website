About us

Serious compliance expertise behind every check.

AML Dealcheck is a product of Compliance On Demand — founded and led by Philip Olmer, one of the UK real estate sector's most experienced compliance practitioners.

Philip Olmer

Solicitor, Director & Founder

Philip has over 35 years' experience as a solicitor in the real estate industry. For more than 25 years he was a partner in the City law firms Berwin Leighton, Olswang and Nabarro (now part of CMS).

In 2014 he joined a leading global real estate asset manager as head of legal and compliance in London. In 2018, with Brexit a fait accompli, he set up Compliance On Demand, having identified a need for tailored compliance and regulatory support for the real estate sector. The firm now counts LSE-listed REITs, FCA-regulated investment managers, ultra-high-net-worth investors and some of the best-known private fund managers and estate agencies among its clients.

Philip holds an SRA practising certificate and is approved by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) to serve as Compliance Officer and Anti-Money Laundering Officer for FCA-regulated fund managers — a role he holds for Orchard Street Investment Management.

He also sits on the British Property Federation Regulatory Committee, and Compliance On Demand is a proud member of the BPF.
