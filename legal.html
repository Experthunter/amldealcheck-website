Privacy Policy

AML Dealcheck is a product of Compliance On Demand ("we", "us", "our"). We are the data controller for personal data processed through this website and the AML Dealcheck system. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in line with the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

What we collect

  • Details you submit through our demo-request and contact forms (name, company, email, phone, role and message).
  • Information you and your users enter into the AML Dealcheck system in the course of AML due diligence.
  • Limited technical and analytics data, only where you have given consent (see Cookies below).

Lawful basis

We process demo and contact data on the basis of our legitimate interest in responding to your enquiry and, where applicable, the steps necessary to enter into a contract. Data processed within the AML Dealcheck system is handled on behalf of our client under a data-processing agreement, for the purpose of meeting the client's anti-money-laundering obligations.

Hosting & security

AML Dealcheck is hosted on the Microsoft Azure Southern England secure platform and is built to meet UK regulatory and data-protection requirements. Fonts and assets on this website are self-hosted, so no third-party requests are made before you give consent.

Retention

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes described above and to meet our legal, regulatory and record-keeping obligations, after which it is securely deleted.

Cookies

This website sets no cookies and runs no analytics until you opt in via the cookie banner. The only pre-consent storage is a record of your cookie choice, which is strictly necessary and does not itself require consent. You can change your choice at any time using the "Cookie settings" link in the footer.
